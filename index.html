Corruption is a serious problem in Cambodia - both from the perspective of having a functioning society and from a sheer inconvenience factor. CRAP seeks to help address these issues by providing a simple interface for its two main functions: reporting and plotting.

Reporting

Corruption can be reported automatically by type and bribe paid with location data collected automatically from your smartphone.

Plotting

Did you forget your helmet? Are you riding three to a moto? Or are you just interested in where the police are stopping people today? Check out the heat-map of the city and avoid those tricky intersections.

You can see just today's problem spots, or view historical data to figure out your best route to work.

Just want to know how big the problem is? Check out the daily, monthly and all time dollar figures! You can even download the data to analyze yourself.
